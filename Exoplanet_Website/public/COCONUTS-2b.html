COCONUTS-2b

COCONUTS-2 b is a gas giant exoplanet that orbits an M-type star. Its mass is 6.3 Jupiters, it takes 1101369.9 years to complete one orbit of its star, and is 7506.0 AU from its star. Its discovery was announced in 2021.
